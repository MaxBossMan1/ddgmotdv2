Welcome to DigitalDeltaGaming!

Please read all rules carefully before playing.

General Rules

Rule 1: No RDM (Random Death Match) - Do not kill players without a valid RP reason.
Rule 2: No breaking character - Stay in character at all times.
Rule 3: Respect all players and staff members.
Rule 4: No mic spam or chat spam.

NLR (New Life Rule)

NLR Timer: 3 minutes - You cannot return to the area where you died.
Memory Loss: Forget everything from your previous life.

Warning System

1-2 Warns: Verbal warning from staff
3-4 Warns: 1 day ban
5+ Warns: 3 day ban or longer
