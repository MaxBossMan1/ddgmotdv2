Welcome to DigitalDeltaGaming!

Please read all rules carefully before playing. Breaking rules will result in punishment.

General Rules

1. No RDM (Random Death Match)
Do not kill players without a valid roleplay reason.
2. No RDA (Random Arrest)
Police cannot arrest without valid reason.
3. Stay In Character
No breaking character or talking about out-of-game topics.
4. No Mic/Chat Spam
Do not spam voice chat or text chat.
5. Respect All Players
Be respectful to all players and staff members.

NLR (New Life Rule)

NLR Timer: 3 Minutes
You cannot return to the area where you died for 3 minutes.
Forget Previous Life
You must forget everything from your previous life.
No Revenge Killing
You cannot kill someone who killed you in your previous life.

Warning System

1-2 Warns: Verbal warning from staff
3-4 Warns: 1 day ban
5-6 Warns: 3 day ban
7+ Warns: 1 week ban or permanent ban

Job-Specific Rules

Police/Government:
- Must follow law and government rules
- Cannot abuse power or randomly arrest
- Must have valid reason for searches
Criminals:
- Cannot kill other criminals without reason
- Must advert before raiding
- Cannot camp spawn areas
Citizens:
- Cannot own illegal weapons
- Must follow all laws
- Can call police for help
