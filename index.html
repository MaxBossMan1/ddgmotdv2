DIGITAL DELTA GAMING

Welcome to DigitalDeltaGaming!

Please read all rules carefully before playing. Not following the rules may result in punishment.

General Rules

1. Use common sense.
Think before you act. If something seems like it would ruin someone's experience, don't do it.
2. If you find yourself using a "loophole" in the rules to your advantage, it is likely you are breaking at least one other rule.
Don't try to exploit rule technicalities. Follow the spirit of the rules, not just the letter.
3. No politics, harassment, sexism, racism, discrimination, bigotry, porn, NSFW content, malicious behavior, etc.
Keep the server friendly and welcoming for everyone.
4. Crossfire is counted as RDM.
Be careful when shooting - you're responsible for where your bullets go.
5. Running into spawn to avoid any roleplay situation is considered FailRP.
Don't use spawn as a safe zone during roleplay scenarios.
6. No class can /advert murder or /advert counter.
These adverts are not allowed for any job class.
7. No propspam, propminges, propsurf, propclimb, propblock, bodyblock etc.
Don't abuse props or block players/areas with your body.
8. Do not FailRP and/or FearRP.
Act realistically and show appropriate fear when threatened.
9. "FearRP is doing something roleplay wise out of fear (e.g. someone is pointing a gun at you, you are not allowed to take out a weapon or run away). "FailRP is doing something against the rules or your job rules/description.
Understand the difference between FearRP and FailRP situations.
10. Voice-changers aren't allowed.
Use your natural voice for roleplay.
11. No mass RPbinds, all binds must specify one action, you cannot bind text too.
Keep binds simple and specific to single actions.
12. Self supplying weapons by spawning as a job with a desired weapon and dropping it is considered FailRP.
Don't exploit job switching to get weapons.
13. Do not bait and or scam players. (In-game, Donation wise)
Be honest in all your dealings with other players.

NLR (New Life Rule)

NLR Timer: 3 Minutes
You cannot return to the area where you died for 3 minutes.
Forget Previous Life
You must forget everything from your previous life including who killed you and why.
No Revenge Killing
You cannot kill someone who killed you in your previous life.
NLR Area
The NLR area extends in a reasonable radius around where you died.

Basing Rules

No Crouch/Jump Bases
Bases that require crouching or jumping to navigate are not allowed.
No Maze Bases
Bases cannot be designed as confusing mazes.
Maximum 3 Fading Doors
You can have a maximum of 3 fading doors in your base.
No Pixel Gaps
Don't create tiny gaps that are hard to see but allow shooting.
No Sky Bases
Bases must be built on solid ground, not floating in the air.

Warning System

1-2 Warnings
Verbal warning from staff member
3-4 Warnings
1 day ban from the server
5-6 Warnings
3 day ban from the server
7+ Warnings
1 week ban or permanent ban depending on severity

Job-Specific Rules

Police/Government Jobs
Must follow all laws and government procedures. Cannot abuse power or randomly arrest citizens. Must have valid reason for searches and arrests.
Criminal Jobs
Cannot kill other criminals without valid reason. Must advert before raiding. Cannot camp spawn areas or government buildings.
Citizen Jobs
Cannot own illegal weapons without proper licenses. Must follow all server laws. Can call police for assistance.
Special Jobs
Each special job has specific rules listed in their job description. Read carefully before playing.

Common RP Terminology

RDM - Random Death Match
Killing someone without a valid roleplay reason.
RDA - Random Arrest
Arresting someone without a valid reason.
FailRP - Fail Roleplay
Acting in a way that breaks roleplay or doesn't make sense.
FearRP - Fear Roleplay
Acting appropriately when your character should be afraid.
NLR - New Life Rule
Forgetting your previous life after dying.
Metagaming
Using information your character wouldn't know in roleplay.
